Order Status

When you place your order, it will go through a series of steps during fulfillment.
  1. 1. Pending: Your order is submitted to us but without checkout.
  2. 2. Confirmed: Your order is accepted by us.
  3. 3. Processing: Your order is under Processing.
  4. 4. Shipped: Your order is collected for assignment by the forwarder.
  5. 5. Traceable: Tracking information for your order is available.
  6. 6. Waiting: Human interaction is needed for your order.
  7. 7. In Production: Your order needs more than 3 working days Processing time.
  8. 8. Canceled: Your order is canceled.

Remark

  1. 1. We will reserve the "pending" order for 2 weeks. If you don't check out within this Period, the order will be canceled.
  2. 2. Once your order has reached "processing" status, it can no longer be canceled or otherwise altered in any way.
  3. 3. It normally takes 1-2 working days for the order status to change from "Shipped" to "Traceable", since we need some time to collect the tracking information from the forwarder and upload it to our system. You can enter your order here to track it when its status reaches "Traceable".
  4. 4. If your order is set as "Waiting" status, our Customer-Service stuff will contact you within 24-48 working hours to get this flag cleared ASAP.
  5. 5. Some Particular orders' Processing time will be longer than 3 working days, like the Fusion Service. We will apply the "In Production" status flag to these orders.
  6. 6. Please contact our customer service stuff timely if you need to cancel an order, and that order doesn't reach the "processing" status. To avoid any trouble, Please recheck your shopping cart before checkout.

Customs, Duties and Taxes

You should expect to pay any amount charged by the government in your respective country. This includes, and is not limited to, duties, taxes and any extra fees charged by the courier company. We will not hold responsible for any extra charges once the original package has been shipped. If the customer refuses to pay these extra charges, the return shipping and any additional fees will be taken out of the cost of the order, with any remaining funds being refunded to the customer.
Customs are quite different in each country. please comment while you are placing order about the requirements, we will support as much as possible.

Split Orders

To fill your order as quickly and efficiently as possible, we sometimes will split your order into a number of different packages so that we could take advantage of the items in stock at different warehouses. For example, we have a warehouse in America so you may receive multiple deliveries if you use American address for shipping.
Regardless of the number of deliveries you receive, you'll only be charged the amount that was originally quoted to you at the time you placed your order.

No returns will be accepted without having previously obtained a return authorization. All returns must be in its original mint and clean condition. Returns on damaged, scratched, programmed, burnt, or otherwise 'played with' product will not be accepted. The product must then be returned in its original packaging with its receipt and a small note for the reason of the return. The note will help us to serve you better and improve our products. Reimbursement will be denied for any products which do not conform to the abovementioned conditions.
Please note that all items will be reimbursed within 3 days of receiving the product (shipping fees excluded). You must cover the cost of returning the product. You will need to pay for shipping (if you want a refund instead of a replacement). Shipping fees will only be refunded if the return is a result of a shipping error on our part.
